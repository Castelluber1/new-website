FAQ

What if I don’t know where to start?

That’s exactly why this consultation exists. We’ll help you understand your real options and guide you toward the programs that fit your case and goals.

What if I’m not eligible right now?

We’ll show you what applies now and how you can improve. Many clients come to us before they’re fully ready. That’s how they build a plan to get ready.

Do I need to speak English for the session?

No. You can choose Portuguese or English, whichever you’re most comfortable with.

I don’t have money to immigrate right now. Is this still worth it?

If you’re serious about immigrating in the future, yes. Even if you’re not applying soon, this session helps you plan ahead, save time, and avoid costly mistakes.

Does the consultation include the application too?

The consultation is a one-on-one strategy session. If you decide to move forward, we credit the full amount toward your application.

I’ve already done a lot of research. Will this help me?

Absolutely. With so much information online, it's hard to know what is accurate. We’ll validate what you’ve found, highlight gaps, and help you what matters.

Will I talk to Larissa directly?

Yes. All consultations are conducted by Larissa Castelluber, a licensed RCIC with years of experience.

What if I want to apply on my own after the session?

That’s totally fine. You can take the advice and use it however you choose no obligation to hire us for the application.
