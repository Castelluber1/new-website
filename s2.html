Can You Sponsor Your Spouse?

Quick checks that help you decide your path and timeline.

Who qualifies

Eligible sponsors (Canadian citizen or PR) and applicants in a genuine marriage, common-law, or conjugal relationship who meet medical/security rules.

We review red flags early so you don’t waste time or risk refusals.

Basic requirements

  • Proof of a genuine relationship
  • Sponsor eligible (not barred from sponsoring)
  • Complete forms, police, biometrics, medicals
  • Consistent dates, addresses, travel, status

Frequently asked questions

Do we apply inland or outland?

It depends on your travel needs, location, and timing. We help you choose the stream that fits your situation.

What relationship proof should we include?

Marriage/common-law docs, leases, finances, travel, photos, messages, and affidavits organized into a clear, consistent story.

Can the sponsored spouse work while PR is processing?

Some inland cases may qualify for an open work permit. We confirm eligibility based on current rules.

How long does it take?

Timelines vary by stream and visa office. We set expectations and structure your file to avoid preventable delays.

What causes refusals?

Weak relationship evidence, inconsistencies, missing documents, or eligibility issues. We design your file to address IRCC concerns.

Can we include dependent children?

Yes, if they meet the definition of dependent. We include custody docs, medicals, and forms as required.
