Best Province for Newcomers: Why 82% of Immigrants Stay in Ontario

5 min read • Feb 20, 2026

Ontario has consistently been the top destination for Canadian immigrants, with an 82% retention rate among newcomers. From job opportunities to cultural diversity, discover why Ontario is the best province for newcomers looking to build their life in Canada.

Why Ontario Leads for Newcomers

Ontario's popularity among immigrants is no coincidence. The province offers a unique combination of economic opportunities, cultural diversity, and quality of life that makes it an ideal destination for newcomers. With an 82% retention rate, it's clear that immigrants who choose Ontario tend to stay and build their futures here.

Key Statistics

Understanding the numbers behind Ontario's appeal helps paint a picture of why so many newcomers choose this province:

  • 82% of immigrants who settle in Ontario remain in the province
  • Toronto alone receives approximately 35% of all Canadian immigrants annually
  • Over 30% of Ontario's population is foreign-born
  • The province is home to over 200 immigrant communities
  • Average salary for skilled workers in Ontario is among the highest in Canada

Why Ontario is the Best Choice

Economic Opportunities

Ontario's economy is the largest in Canada, with a diverse range of industries including finance, technology, healthcare, manufacturing, and services. Toronto is a global financial hub, and the tech scene in the city rivals Silicon Valley. Whether you're in IT, finance, healthcare, or skilled trades, Ontario offers abundant employment opportunities and competitive wages.

Diverse Communities

Ontario is incredibly multicultural. Toronto, in particular, is one of the most diverse cities in the world. You'll find communities from virtually every corner of the globe, making it easier to maintain cultural connections while integrating into Canadian society. Whether you're looking for familiar cuisine, cultural events, or places of worship, you'll find them in Ontario.

Quality Education

Ontario has excellent schools and universities, including the University of Toronto, McMaster University, and Western University. If you have children, they'll have access to high-quality education. The province also has robust English Language Learning (ELL) programs in schools to support students who are learning English as a second language.

Quality of Life

Ontario offers a high quality of life with excellent healthcare, public transportation, and recreational facilities. The province has a well-funded healthcare system, and most newcomers have access to government-funded settlement services that help with housing, employment, and language training.

Cultural Scene

From world-class museums and galleries to theater productions, sporting events, and music festivals, Ontario has a vibrant cultural scene. Cities like Toronto and Ottawa offer endless entertainment and cultural opportunities that appeal to people of all ages and interests.

Next Steps

If you're considering moving to Ontario, the next step is to understand your immigration options. Whether you're coming as a skilled worker, international student, or family sponsorship candidate, Ontario offers pathways for newcomers at all stages of their careers and life circumstances.
