Find answers to common questions about your application

To start your application, you'll need:

  • Valid passport or travel document
  • Birth certificate
  • Educational credentials
  • Employment history and references
  • Proof of financial stability
  • Medical examination results (if required)
  • Police clearance certificate

Our team will guide you through each requirement step by step.

The timeline varies depending on your visa type and circumstances, but typically:

  • Documentation Review: 5-7 days
  • Initial Assessment: 7-10 days
  • Interview (if required): 2-3 weeks
  • Final Decision: 2-5 days

You can track your progress in real-time on your dashboard.

Limited changes can be made after submission:

  • During the documentation phase, you can update information
  • After initial assessment, changes may require re-review
  • Contact our support team immediately if you need to make updates
  • Some fields cannot be changed once locked

We recommend careful review before final submission.

We accept the following file formats:

  • PDF: Preferred format for all documents
  • Images: JPG, PNG (max 5MB per file)
  • Documents: DOC, DOCX
  • Scanned: Must be clear and legible (300 DPI minimum)

Maximum file size: 10MB. Total upload limit: 500MB.

Documents may be marked as missing for several reasons:

  • The file hasn't been uploaded yet
  • The file format is not acceptable
  • The document is illegible or incomplete
  • The document has expired
  • Additional proof is required for verification

Check the document details for specific instructions on what's needed.

Your documents are protected with:

  • Encryption: All uploads are encrypted end-to-end
  • Secure Storage: Documents stored on secure servers
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can view
  • Audit Trail: All access is logged and monitored

We comply with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
