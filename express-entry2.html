Why this happens

Express Entry calculates a CRS score using age, education, work history, and language. Without a proper review, people make choices that lower their score or delay their plan.

  • Missed points from language or ECA
  • Wrong assumptions about work experience
  • No plan for provincial options or spousal factors

How Express Entry works

A selection system that manages three federal pathways

For professionals with foreign work experience.

Canadian Experience Class

For those who worked or studied in Canada.

For technical and trade workers.

Common questions

Short answers for faster decisions

What is a good CRS score

It depends on the draw type and program. The goal is to lift your score to sit above recent cutoffs or to add a provincial option that issues a nomination.

Can I apply with my spouse

Yes. Spousal factors can add points or change strategy. We review both profiles to choose the lead applicant and the best plan.

How long does it take

Timelines vary. The fastest path is to improve your score before you enter the pool, so you are ready when an invite comes.
